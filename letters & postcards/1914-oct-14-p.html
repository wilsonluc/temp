Post Card. Thursday Sept. 3rd. 1914.

Just a few lines to let you know that everything is alright on the “Omrah”. The latest we have heard is that we will be here until about the 18th. Oct.. It is awfully hot here during the day but nice and cool at night. When we get out on leave we always manage to have a good time in Melbourne - this should be our night off but it is our turn to go on guard so we will have to wait. The New Zealand troops have not arrived yet, but are expected any time although some say they may not come here but go straight to Albany. But we cannot believe anything we hear as there are so many yarns going about. Hope that letter I wrote arrived safely, I did not post it myself. How are all my friends? Hope all are in the best of health at home.

Love from Ray.
